# Privacy Policy

*Effective date: 2026-06-25*

AICatchy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AICatchy web application at https://aicatchy.com (the "Service"). This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP, Law No. 27 of 2022).

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data needed to generate outfit recommendations and improve the Service. We deliberately collect as little as possible.

### 1.1 Data You Provide Directly

| Data | When Collected | Purpose |
|------|---------------|---------|
| Occasion description | You tell us what event you're dressing for (e.g., "Date Night," "Kondangan") | Generate outfit recommendations |
| Vibe keywords | You pick 1-3 mood words (e.g., "elegant," "playful") | Fine-tune style |
| Expression preference | You choose a style archetype (e.g., "Daring") | Match recommendations to your identity |
| Shopping intent | You indicate whether you want to buy or just explore | Route to affiliate links or inspiration mode |
| Height, Body shape | You optionally provide these for fit accuracy | Improve outfit relevance |
| Body-fit notes | You optionally share fit or silhouette preferences (e.g., "prefer high-rise pants," "broad shoulders") | Guide fit suggestions — **never** used for beauty or identity inference |

#### 1.1.1 How Body Data is Handled

- **Optional:** All body-related data is optional and explicitly volunteered.
- **Purpose-bound:** Used solely to generate better-fitting outfit recommendations.
- **No inference:** We never use body data for beauty scores, identity classification, health inference, or any purpose beyond outfit fit.
- **No sharing:** Body data is never shared with third parties, including affiliate networks.
- **Retention:** Body data is retained only while your session is active. If saved to a profile, it remains there until you delete it.

### 1.2 Data Collected Automatically

| Data | Why |
|------|-----|
| Referral cookie (from affiliate link) | Track purchases for commission attribution (7-day window) |
| Basic usage analytics (page views, feature usage) | Improve the Service |
| Device & browser info (user-agent, screen size) | Optimize display |
| Approximate location (country-level from IP) | Comply with Indonesia law and show relevant local brands |

#### 1.2.1 Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

- **Affiliate referral cookies:** Set when you arrive via an affiliate link. Expire after 7 days. Used solely for purchase attribution.
- **Session cookies:** Used to maintain your current outfit preferences during a visit. No persistent tracking.

You can disable cookies in your browser, but affiliate attribution may not work correctly and your experience may be less seamless.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Legal Basis (UU PDP) |
|---------|---------------------|
| Generate outfit recommendations | Contract performance (Art. 16) — you ask, we deliver |
| Attribute affiliate purchases | Legitimate interest (Art. 17) — paying our bills keeps the Service free |
| Improve recommendations and fix issues | Consent (Art. 18) — you agree when using the Service; you may opt out anytime |
| Comply with Indonesian law (privacy, anti-terrorism financing, Kominfo standards, sharia compliance) | Legal obligation (Art. 19) |

We do **not** use your data for:
- Advertising or ad targeting
- Selling personal data
- Training AI/ML models (LLM routing uses no training on user data)
- Automated individual decision-making with legal effects

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We share data only as necessary to provide the Service:

| Recipient | What | Why | Safeguards |
|-----------|------|-----|------------|
| LLM provider | Occasion, vibe, expression, height, body shape, body-fit notes | Generate outfit recommendations | [OpenAI API](https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policy) — no training on API inputs; data not used for model improvement |
| Affiliate networks (Shopee, Involve Asia) | Referral cookie, click/purchase event | Earn commission to keep the Service free | Short-lived cookie; no personal data shared voluntarily |
| Analytics service | Aggregated event counts (page views, feature usage) | Improve the Service | Anonymized; no personal data |
| Government authorities | As required by Indonesian law | Comply with legal obligations | Full legal review before any disclosure |

We do **not** share data with any other third parties.

### 3.1 International Transfers

Data may be processed outside Indonesia (e.g., OpenAI's US-based API). We rely on:
- Adequacy determination by Kominfo (where applicable)
- Standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards
- Your consent to processing necessary for Service delivery

## 4. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed:

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Session data | Duration of your visit |
| Affiliate referral cookie | 7 days |
| Saved looks (if you create a profile) | Until you delete them |
| Analytics aggregates | 24 months (rolling) |
| Legal/compliance data | As required by Indonesian law |

## 5. Your Rights (UU PDP)

Under Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law, you have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|---------------|
| **Access** — Know what data we hold about you | Email privacy@aicatchy.com |
| **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data | Update your profile or email us |
| **Erasure** — Delete your data | Email privacy@aicatchy.com (we respond within 3×24 hours) |
| **Restriction** — Limit processing | Email privacy@aicatchy.com |
| **Portability** — Receive your data in a structured format | Email privacy@aicatchy.com |
| **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest | Email privacy@aicatchy.com |
| **Withdraw consent** — At any time, without affecting prior processing | Adjust preferences or email us |

We respond to all requests within 3×24 hours as required by UU PDP.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS / TLS 1.3)
- **No storage** of sensitive personal data beyond what you explicitly save
- **Minimal data philosophy** — we collect the least data needed
- **Access control** — only the Service systems process your data; no human access except for legally compelled disclosure
