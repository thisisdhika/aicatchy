# Affiliate Disclosure

*Legal compliance for affiliate marketing in Indonesia — user-facing disclosure requirements.*

---

## 1. Applicable Regulations

AICatchy earns affiliate commissions when users purchase products through Shopee or participating Involve Asia merchant links. The following regulations govern disclosure requirements:

| Regulation | Scope | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| UU No. 8/1999 (Perlindungan Konsumen) | Consumer protection, deceptive advertising | Users must not be misled about commercial relationships |
| UU No. 19/2016 (UU ITE) | Electronic transactions, advertising | Commercial electronic communications must be identifiable |
| Peraturan BPKN No. 1/2013 | Advertising ethics in Indonesia | Sponsored/affiliate content must be labelled |
| Shopee Affiliate Program Terms | Platform-specific | Affiliate links must be disclosed per platform policy |
| Involve Asia advertiser / campaign terms | Platform-specific | Affiliate links must follow the active campaign's disclosure policy |

**Secondary market consideration:** If AICatchy expands outside Indonesia, apply local disclosure equivalents (FTC Endorsement Guides for US, ASA CAP Code for UK, ACCC for Australia) before entering market.

---

## 2. Disclosure Text

### 2.1 In-App (Web App — MLP)

Displayed once per session on first recommendation screen, and on any screen with affiliate links.

> Bahasa Indonesia:
> "Beberapa link adalah link afiliasi. AICatchy mendapat komisi tanpa biaya tambahan untukmu."

> English equivalent (shown if device language is non-Indonesian):
> "Some links are affiliate links. AICatchy earns a commission at no extra cost to you."

### 2.2 WhatsApp Concierge Messages

Included in the first message of every concierge delivery. Must be visible without scrolling.

> "Hi [nama]! Berikut rekomendasi outfit dari AICatchy untuk acara [occasion]. Beberapa link yang dishare adalah link afiliasi — AICatchy mendapat komisi tanpa biaya tambahan untukmu. Yuk, coba lihat!"

### 2.3 Social Media Shares

When a user shares a verdict or outfit card to social media (Instagram, WhatsApp groups, Twitter), the share card must include:

> "Affiliate link — AICatchy may earn commission."

In Indonesian if the share context is Indonesian:

> "Link afiliasi — AICatchy bisa mendapat komisi."

### 2.4 Email (Future Channel)

Header or footer of any email containing product recommendations:

> "Beberapa produk di email ini mengandung link afiliasi. AICatchy mendapat komisi tanpa biaya tambahan untukmu."

## 3. Placement Requirements

| Channel | Location | Minimum visibility |
|---|---|---|
| In-app | Below the "3 outfit recommendations" heading, before individual item links | Same font size as body text, full opacity, no CSS hiding |
| WhatsApp | First message paragraph, before outfit cards | Full-width text, readable typeface (no smaller than other message text) |
| Social share | Bottom of the share card image | Minimum 8pt on standard card (600×900px), contrasting background |
| Email (future) | Header or footer | Same font size as body text |

**Timing:** Disclosure must be visible before the user clicks any affiliate link. In the web app, disclosure renders before purchase links are actionable. In WhatsApp, disclosure precedes link delivery in the same message.

**Prohibited practices:**
- Placing disclosure below the fold or behind an expand/collapse toggle
- Using disclosure text smaller than the smallest body text
- Using colors that blend into the background (minimum WCAG AA contrast)
- Fading disclosure after a timer

---

## 4. Consequences of Non-Compliance

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Shopee/Involve Asia campaign suspension | Loss of affiliate revenue stream, reputational damage | Review disclosure on every platform policy update |
| Consumer complaint (BPKN or YLKI) | Investigation, fines, public notice | Maintain disclosure audit log; respond to complaints within 7 days |
| User trust erosion | Churn, negative social sharing | Proactive transparency; never obscure commercial relationship |
| Regulatory penalty (UU ITE) | Fines up to Rp 1B, criminal liability for deliberate omission | Annual legal review; counsel sign-off on any disclosure change |
