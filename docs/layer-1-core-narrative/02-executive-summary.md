# AICatchy: Executive Summary

*AI Fashion Decision Product — June 2026*

**Tagline:** TikTok inspires. Shopee sells. AICatchy decides.

---

## Problem

Indonesian Gen Z and young Millennials have infinite access to fashion products — Shopee, TikTok Shop, Instagram — and no tool that helps them decide. The typical journey: scroll 45 minutes, buy nothing or the wrong thing, ask a WhatsApp group, repeat. The bottleneck shifted from supply to decision. Nobody owns that moment.

## Product

AICatchy is an AI personal stylist that operates with **stylist-level confidence at product-level speed**. Users describe an occasion (tap a chip or type freely), a vibe, and their shopping intent. AICatchy returns complete, magazine-worthy (elevated but wearable) outfits — Safe, Stylish, Bolder — each with an explainable styling note on *why it works for this occasion, this person*. Total time: under 2 minutes.

**The Hook (Earned Auth):** The core experience starts without logging in. Auth happens *after* the first recommendation, driven by the desire to save a look. The hook is simple: *"Save this look, and let's get to know each other."* Pre-login saves are held locally first, then claimed into the new account.

**Memory & Personalization:** Accounts unlock server-side persistence and long-term memory: occasion history, saved looks, and an evolving style profile. The immediate post-signup profile asks only for a name, an optional style-preference starter, and an optional body-fit note. Body data is used strictly for fit/silhouette guidance, never beauty or identity inference. The stylist provides wardrobe-compatible suggestions, rather than attempting full wardrobe modeling. Personalization is visible but light. Non-logged-in users can continue in a limited non-persistent mode.

## Why Now

Indonesian fashion e-commerce is $10.35B (2026), growing to $12.16B. Gen Z is mobile-first and chronically underserved at the decision layer. TikTok and Shopee invested billions in discovery and supply. Neither invested in *helping you decide*. The decision moment is open, and the window will not stay open.

## Target

Indonesian Gen Z and young Millennials (18–30). Mobile-first, WhatsApp-native, price-sensitive (Rp 100K–500K per outfit). Publicly inclusive — speaks to anyone who's stood in front of a closet thinking "I have nothing to wear." Not fashion enthusiasts or people who enjoy browsing. People who want to be dressed, not to shop.

## Wedge

Existing platforms own supply (Shopee), discovery (TikTok), or social identity (Instagram). None outfit items across brands for a specific occasion. AICatchy owns the *decision moment*, turning **inspiration overload into a specific, occasion-ready outfit decision you can actually shop**.

## Differentiation

| They own...                     | AICatchy owns...                      |
|---------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| Items and price comparison      | Complete outfit systems               |
| Viral discovery and live commerce | Decision logic per occasion         |
| Social identity and aspiration  | Verdicts you can purchase             |

Trust is the moat, and it comes from human-curated outfit logic plus explainable styling. Every recommendation explains its reasoning. Three options keep the user in control. Editorial quality earns authority.

## Monetization Thesis

AICatchy is an affiliate publisher: affiliate commissions from Shopee Affiliate Program, Shopee AMS (seller-side commission and pay-per-post tool), and Involve Asia merchant links from Day 1 — zero friction, aligned incentives, mature infrastructure in Indonesia. Brand sponsorships are an opportunistic supplement post-validation (Month 3+), not a primary revenue target. Team: 3–5 people, fully remote. Monthly burn: Rp 60–120M. Seed capital: ~$55K–75K for 12-month runway.

## Validation & Scope

Core thesis validated through tester purchases of a cheap product — willingness to transact on recommendations is confirmed (stronger signal than stated preference). A 3-week concierge sprint (15–20 manual sessions via WhatsApp) further tests purchase signal, repeat intent, and trust at scale. Go/Soft-Launch/No-Go decision based on clear thresholds. Early stage: no platform built yet.

**MLP Scope:** The Minimum Lovable Product (MLP) centers on the earned-auth loop and baseline memory. It includes post-result authentication, long-term memory (saves, preferences), and optional body-fit notes. Advanced features like swap items and budget adjustments are explicitly deferred as post-MLP candidates.

## What Success Looks Like

A repeat-use habit: the user's first thought before any occasion is "let me check AICatchy." Verdicts shared to group chats free-acquire the next user. An outfit formula library that compounds — more occasions, better recommendations, stronger moat. Unit of value delivered: **saving 45 minutes of browsing and the regret of buying wrong.**
